"GOA is about RELAXED UNIQUE ENJOYMENT rather than running from one beach to another. Also the host POONAM & PASCAL have designed refreshments like KEESH (salty pie) & unique burgers apart from cold drinks etc. which was enjoyed watching sunset and occasional large group of birds passing by. Thanks to team KONKAN EXPLORERS--A group of nice people to enjoy with........"
